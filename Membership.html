Corporate Membership

Price (per annum):
Individual - £50.00

Worcester Civic Society warmly welcomes corporate members from the business, the public and voluntary sectors operating within the city.
It is a means of the organisation expressing pride in Worcester’s past history, maintaining a keen interest in present day issues affecting the city and wishing to contribute to the debate about enhancing Worcester’s status and well-being for the future.
Being a member of the Society can be a positive reflection of the organisation’s own culture and ethos as being outward looking and supportive of its commitment to Worcester.



Student Membership

Price (per annum):
Individual - £5.00

Worcester Civic Society have access to a wealth of information and photographs relating to the conservation, architecture and culture of our historic city which may be useful to students studying a variety of subjects ranging from classical studies such as English and history, practical such as town planning and architecture and social history in studying how the city has evolved.
These resources and other benefits are available to members of the Worcester Civic Society. We offer students in full time education a special annual membership rate of £5.00
